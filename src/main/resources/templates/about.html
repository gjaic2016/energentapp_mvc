ABOUT

Founded in the revolutionary year of 1920, "Energent" is a steadfast advocate for the principles of hard work. For over a century, we have unwaveringly upheld the ideals of collective ownership, worker solidarity, and the elimination of class distinctions.

At our core, we are committed to dismantling the oppressive forces of capitalism and striving for a society where wealth and resources are shared equitably among all members. Our legacy is one of challenging the status quo, fighting for workers' rights, and championing the marginalized.

Throughout our history, we've proudly contributed to the struggles for workers' rights and social justice. Our firm has played a pivotal role in labor movements, advocating for fair wages, safe working conditions, and the abolition of exploitation.
